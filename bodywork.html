Bodywork

Philip is a City and County of San Francisco licensed Massage Practitioner and Certified Shiatsu Massage Therapist with additional training in positional release techniques.

This enhanced training added to his fitness expertise allows him help clients who may only need bodywork therapy. Clients have found his style of bodywork helpful in addressing acute or chronic issues of the neck, back, shoulders, hips or knees. He has over 5,000 hours of bodywork experience.

By incorporating varying degrees of the following therapy styles in his sessions, he can meet his client's needs more effectively.

Shiatsu

Shiatsu is Japanese for "finger pressure." It is a massage therapy style developed in Japan and based on the Chinese medical theory that disease and pain are caused by blocked qi (life force, energy; pronounced "chee") along energy pathways in the body. Using Shiatsu techniques to application of pressure to the blocked meridian could provide relief from pain and disease.

Positional Release

Positional release (counterstrain) is an osteopathy based form of manual therapy which benefits those with chronic stress, injuries and pain. Gentle movements and body positions are used with tender points to encourage positive changes in stress and pain patterns. Positional release therapy can help alleviate certain types of acute and chronic muscle pain while relaxing the entire body.
